Yang Zhou

Associate Professor, Master/PhD Supervisor

Shanghai University

About

Prof. Zhou Yang is the main leader of the intelligent perception area at the Unmanned Surface Vehicle (USV) team in Shanghai University, and a core member in the key research areas and major projects team of ocean intelligent unmanned systems and AI for Science., with a multidisciplinary academic background in mechanical engineering, mechanics, ocean engineering, image processing and artificial intelligence. He is currently engaged in theoretical and applied research in the field of intelligent perception for unmanned systems, also responsible for undergraduate and graduate courses on artificial intelligence algorithms, deep learning, digital image processing, and related subjects.

Interests
  • Visual information processing under marine environment
  • Multimodal collaborative perception in unmanned systems
  • Multi-modal fusion perception in graphics, text and audio
